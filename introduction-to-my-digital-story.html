About Me
Hi!  my name is Mya! I am an adventure, nature lover, and above all life long learner.   I teach individuals the necessities of getting started on their first backpacking trip and how beginners can become backpacking pros!  I have a love for travel and experiencing the world through backpacking.   Whether you need guidance on your first backpacking trip or looking for inspiration, you have come to the right destination.  

This blog will specifically focus on all of California's wonders and great places for beginners to get started on their first trip into the wild.  I am always searching for new knowledge, and I am here to share my new knowledge and previous knowledge with you.  Venture on your first backing trip can be scary; you're certain of what will happen.  I am here to be a guide to help answer all the scary unknowns and challenging questions.   Get ready for a wild ready, and let pack our backpacks together and get started!
