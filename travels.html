Travels

Califronia has so maany Amazing places to go backpacking it can be hard to pick just one.   Below are three of the best backpacking sites to get started! 

Eagle Lake & Dick's Lake

1. Frist up is Eagles lake to Dick's lake, this is a great first time backpacker trip.  On this trip you an expect to see stunning views of Lake Tahoe as well as gorgous foliage.  This is a 13 Mile hike with an elevation change 1500ft.  This is a hike you wont forget!

  Cayon Creek's 

2.  Next up on our list is a all time favorite canyon creek.  This is a 15 mile trek that can be split up into two days.  This hike has a elevation change of just over 2000 feet which will surely get your heart rate bumping.  With over 55 alpine lake you will be sure to find something you like! 

Pt. Reyes Coast campground

3.  Every want to spend a night above the sea?  Spend a night on the cliffs on Pt. Reyes and experience one of califronia wonders.  This is a shorter hike of just over 4 mile with an elevation change of 633.  Hike to this campground and tehn take a short walk to the beaches!
